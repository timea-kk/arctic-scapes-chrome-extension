# Privacy Policy — Arctic Scapes

**Last updated: March 2026**

Arctic Scapes is a Chrome extension that replaces your new tab page with curated arctic landscape photography.

## What data we collect

None. Arctic Scapes does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data whatsoever.

## What gets stored on your device

The extension saves a small amount of data locally on your own device — only:

- Your display preferences (clock format, whether the clock/greeting/location are shown)
- The position in the photo sequence, so photos cycle without repeating

This data is stored using Chrome's built-in local storage. It never leaves your device and is never sent anywhere.

## Third-party services

Arctic Scapes loads the **Plus Jakarta Sans** font from Google Fonts when you open a new tab. This is a standard web font request — the same as any website that uses Google Fonts. Google's own privacy policy governs that request. No other third-party services are contacted.

## Photos

All 150 photos are bundled directly inside the extension. No images are fetched from the internet after installation. Photographer credits link to Unsplash when clicked, but those links only open if you choose to click them.
